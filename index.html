Jorge and Poe met in previous musician jobs. They became friends and after a while they thought it would be an awesome idea become a Queen tribute band. They knew a lot of musicians in the Canary Islands, but they needed a queen, couse Jorge played the guitar and Poe was the drummer.

In that moment Juanfran appeared. Suddenly they realized they were ready.
As they play every night in different places, you can see Nestor (trumpet) when they play in the north of the island, Richard (bass) when they play in the east, and Gabriella (chello) when they play in the south (except second Friday of every month).

Controversy

Some photos of Jorge and Poe fighting have been leaked. There´s a video of Jorge saying that he won´t get back to the band. Although Jorge says that he was just kidding, some fans say he was drunk that night, also he had his lips covered in blood.

Juanfran is a motorbiker collector. He is known in his hometown for having the record of penaltee fee for not parking in the right place. He never pays on time, not for not having the money, just couse that´s the only rebellious act he can have.

Jorge´s other group (we are not gonna name it, but most people know about it) at first tried really hard to make Queen tribute tour impossible to Jorge. Making both concerts the same day. Going out to party with him the nigth before a Queen tribute show. But Jorge just said that this was not on purpose, and he just deffenden them, being always by their side.
